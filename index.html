Persona 1

Persona Type: Single Young Adult
Name: Mirel
Age: 24
Location: Miami
Technical Comfort: Advanced
Profession: Junior Software Engineer
"Now that I'm out of school and working I am looking to get out there and find fun things to do and new people to meet!" -Mirel

Backstory

  • Has spent most of his life in education and pursuing internships with limited free time.
  • Recently found employment as a full time junior SWE and with this done he is ready to find new and exciting experiences to enjoy.
  • Likes music, long time interest of going to festivals/concerts.

Motivations

  • Wants to get to know the city he's lived in his whole life but never explored.
  • With a more set-schedule he is looking to structure his life more.
  • He wants to participate in common South Florida activities like a day at the beach.

Frustrations

  • Does not know where to get started/where to find events/fun places to visit.
  • Most of his peers are spread out across the states since he works remote, so he doesn't know anyone locally.
  • Given his profession, he feels he spends too much time indoors.

Ideal Experience

  • Find an event to go to from the website and meet new people there.
  • Make local friends to go to festivals, beach day events, and enjoy what the area has to offer.
  • Spend time outside in the sun.

Persona 2

Persona Type: Mother & Father
Names: Astrid & Karl
Age: 42 & 43
Location: Kalmar, Sweden
Technical Comfort: Moderate
Profession: Healthcare & Banker
"We've been always wanting to visit the United States, Karl suggested we fly to Miami International and go from there." -Astrid

Backstory

  • Both work long hours, mostly indoors.
  • Parents to 3 children.
  • Born and raised in Sweden.

Motivations

  • With the holidays coming they figured now is as good as time as any to travel.
  • Now that their youngest is a bit older they feel more comfortable going on vacations again.
  • They've been wanting to go to the United States, just haven't been sure where exactly.

Frustrations

  • They are busy with day-to-day life and struggle to find the time to plan a vacation.
  • The costs of vacation are of course a question/they need to manage their budget.
  • Safety is also a concern as they are travelling to a place they have never been and don't necessarily know what to expect.

Ideal Experience

  • They are interested in events that are fun for the family where they can spend quality time.
  • They are also interested in child care services/chaperoned children events, so they can get away for a couple of hours.
  • Explore the cultures found in the United States/the melting pot that is South Florida.
